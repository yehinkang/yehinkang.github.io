Ex-Alta 2

2024-08-26
Tags: Cubesat

The Ex-Alta 2 mission was a cubesat designed by Albertasat, a primarily undergraduate student group at the University of Alberta that undertakes cubesat misisons. The Ex-Alta 2 missions was a part of the Canadian Cubesat Project (CCP) initiative by the Canadian Space Agency (CSA).

Iris Imager

The Iris imager was the primary payload of the Ex-Alta 2 mission. The imager was desinged to image in the near infrared (NIR) and the visible spectrum. The collected data can then be used in indices such as NDVI in order to asses the health and the burn susceptibility of forests.

I joined Albertasat in 2021 and joined the Iris Imager team. For the Ex-alta 2 missions prepare drawing packages for the imager as well as with final assembly of the imager.
