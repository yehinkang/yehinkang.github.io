Ex-Alta 3

August 26, 2024
Tags: Cubesat

Ex-alta 3 is the most recent cubesat mission that Albertasat is working on. The primary objective of this mission is to image sea ice and snow/ice cover in the arctic. There is also a secondary objective to monitor space weather

Iris imager

The Iris imager is the main paylaod for the Ex-Alta 3 cubesat. It is a multi-spectral imager that will be used to image the arctic. The imager is designed to be able to image in the visible, near infrared, and the short wave infrared spectrum.
