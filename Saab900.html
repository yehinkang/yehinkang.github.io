1986 Saab 900

In the summer of 2022 I bought a 1986 Saab 900 for $800 and have been working on it since. To get it roadworthy I needed to replace the no longer produced front main seal with one for a Honda bike crankcase. from there I've had to redo the shocks the brakes and the radiator. on top of some others.

With many parts being unavailable it's ben a challenge but it's been bery interesting nonetheless and having an old Saab around has been a good time.
