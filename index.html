Why Engineering Physics?

I've always been interested in being able to bring ideas to life and I believe that engineering physics provides a broad knowledge base to do this. The program combines electrical engineering with advanced physics, and some mechanical engineering, whats more the nanoengineering option also provides insight into nanoscale design.

I believe that these factors makes this program choice a no brainer. Engineering Physics combines the knowledge of electrical systems that an electrical engineer would possess with knowledge of classical, and modern physics that would be required to create systems that bridge the gap between new discoveries in the field of physics with applications in day to day life.
